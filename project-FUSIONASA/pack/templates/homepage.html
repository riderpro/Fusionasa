What FUSIONASA does

Integrating various genomic databases to form a single database where we perform various analysis and predictions as per various requirements.

Future

Fusion-Genetics is a highly scallable noSQL database, maintained in a distributed hadoop environment. Currently, Fusion-Genetics holds the processed alignment files of few homo sapiens, belonging to only two ethnicity. In future, more ethnic groups can be included in order to have a broader view of the annotated results. In addition other omics data can also be included, like proteomics.

Our Aim

The main focus of Fusion-Genetics is to analyse the root cause of the disease cancer with the help of genomics/epigenomics related data, along with NGS nucleotide level information. This may help the medical field to further carry on with some DNA level editing or some drug discovery issues.
