About

At Fusionasa.com, our objective is to analyse the root cause of the disease cancer with the help of genomics/epigenomics related data, along with NGS nucleotide level information. This may help the medical field to further carry on with some DNA level editing or some drug discovery issues.

Fusion-Genetics is a highly scallable noSQL database, integrating genomic/epigenomic databases, along with the integration of the processed information from the raw DNA whole genome sequence (available as .bam files) of several ethnic group of H.Sapiens. In addition, all the NGS raw read sequences, along with its related information generated against each H.Sapiens data has also been integrated and annotated.

    The databases integrated and annotated are categorized as follows:
  • DNA level structural alterations, including the database of genomic variants (DGV) and copy number variation in disease (CNVD).
  • Disease/Cancer related Genes, includes Tumor Associated Gene Database (Tag), Tumor Suppressor Gene Database (TsGene), and Catalogue of Somatic Mutations in Cancer (Cosmic).
  • Genomic and Epigenomic annotations includes human gene nomenclature HGNC database, Chimerdb annotation the fusion genes, and iMETHYL is another integrated database in this regard, that includes the human DNA methylation information.
  • Nucleotide Level annotations using NGS Technology, includes the processed information of binary alignment files along with the extracted information from NGS raw reads of Homo sapiens.
